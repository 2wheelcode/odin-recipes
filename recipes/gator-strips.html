Gator Strips

Make sure it's not playing possum!

I've heard they taste like chicken...

Harvesting Instructions:

  • The skin is tough to cut through
  • Best cut into thin strips
  • Watch your back for other ones nearby...they have big teeth!

Cooking Instructions:

  • Sharpen a stick
  • Weave it back and froth over the sharp end of stick
  • Hold over the fire, like a hot-dog
Serve on a hot-dog bun with mustard and hot-sauce!

*Enjoy at your own risk...
